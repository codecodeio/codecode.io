## Privacy Policy

_Last updated: November 21, 2025_

**CodeCode** (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates a free blog site at [codecode.io](https://codecode.io).

### What Information Do We Collect?

We do **not** collect, store, or track any personal information or usage data from visitors to this website, except if you voluntarily provide your email address (for example, to subscribe or contact us).

### How Is Your Email Used?

If you provide your email address, it is used **only** to send you the requested email via our provider, [Resend](https://resend.com/). We do not store your email address or use it for any other purpose. Resend’s privacy policy applies to the delivery of these emails.

### No Analytics, No Cookies

We do **not** use analytics, tracking scripts, or cookies of any kind. We do not monitor your activity, device, or location.

### No Sale or Distribution of Data

We do **not** sell, rent, share, or distribute any user data to any third parties, except for the email delivery described above.

### Hosting

Our site is hosted by Netlify. Netlify may collect basic technical information (such as IP address) for security and operational purposes. See [Netlify’s privacy policy](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/) for details.

### Children’s Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or process any information from children.

### Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this policy and clearly communicate any changes on this page.

### Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via [X (Twitter)](https://x.com/codecodeio), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/codecode), or [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/codecodeio).
